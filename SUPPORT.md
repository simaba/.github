Use Issues **only** for:
- Reporting errors or inaccuracies in the documentation
- Requesting specific features or additions

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I use these frameworks commercially?**
A: Yes — all repositories are MIT licensed unless otherwise stated.

**Q: Are these frameworks aligned with NIST AI RMF?**
A: Yes, the governance playbook and risk taxonomy explicitly map to the
NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) functions: Govern, Map, Measure, Manage.

**Q: Do these cover EU AI Act requirements?**
A: The frameworks address many EU AI Act compliance requirements, particularly
for high-risk AI systems. See the `docs/eu-ai-act-mapping.md` files where available.

**Q: Can I contribute a case study from my organization?**
A: Absolutely — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidance on submitting
anonymized case studies.
